📁 Projects Overview
1️⃣ Customer Performance Report (https://github.com/navedqureshi0511/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/Customer%20Performance%20Report.pdf)

Objective:
Analyze customer-wise net sales growth over multiple years and identify high-growth customers.

Key Analysis:

Year-wise Net Sales comparison (2019–2021)

Growth percentage analysis (21 vs 20)

Identification of top-performing and emerging customers

Conditional formatting for quick insights

Key Insight Example:

Overall customer sales grew by 304.5% in 2021 vs 2020, indicating aggressive expansion and demand growth.

2️⃣ Market Performance vs Target Report(https://github.com/navedqureshi0511/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/Market%20Performance%20vs%20Target%20Report.pdf)

Objective:
Evaluate country-wise market performance against 2021 targets.

Key Analysis:

Actual vs Target sales variance

Percentage shortfall analysis by country

Identification of underperforming and near-target markets

Key Insight Example:

Despite strong absolute growth, overall markets missed targets by ~9.17%, highlighting forecasting or execution gaps.

3️⃣ Profit & Loss (P&L) Statement – By Fiscal Year(https://github.com/navedqureshi0511/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Fiscal%20Year.pdf)

Objective:
Assess company-wide financial performance and profitability trends.

Key Analysis:

Net Sales, COGS, and Gross Margin comparison

Gross Margin % trend analysis

Year-on-year growth evaluation

Key Insight Example:

Net Sales increased by 204.5% (2021 vs 2020), while Gross Margin % declined, indicating margin pressure due to rising costs.

4️⃣ Profit & Loss (P&L) Statement – By Market(https://github.com/navedqureshi0511/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Markets.pdf)

Objective:
Compare profitability across different countries/markets.

Key Analysis:

Market-wise Net Sales, COGS, and Gross Margin

High-margin vs low-margin market identification

Strategic market comparison

Key Insight Example:

Markets like Japan, UK, and New Zealand show stronger gross margins, making them premium-focus regions.

5️⃣ Profit & Loss (P&L) Statement – Monthly & Quarterly(https://github.com/navedqureshi0511/Excel-Sales-Analytics/blob/main/P%26L%20Statement%20by%20Months.pdf)

Objective:
Analyze seasonal trends and monthly financial performance.

Key Analysis:

Monthly & quarterly sales trends

Seasonal demand patterns

Month-on-month growth comparison

Key Insight Example:

Q3 and Q4 consistently outperform earlier quarters, highlighting seasonality in demand.

📌 Key Business Insights Delivered

Identified high-growth customers and markets

Revealed margin pressure despite revenue growth

Provided data-driven inputs for strategic decision-making
